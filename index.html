About Me

My name is Gouri Sankar A, widely known as Interceptor, a dedicated Cyber Security Researcher, Bug Hunter, Developer, and Programmer based in Kottayam, Kerala.


I completed my schooling at De Paul Public School, Nazarathuhill, Kuravilangad, followed by higher secondary education at Sree Krishna Vocational Higher Secondary School, Kurichitanam.

Currently, I am honing my skills as an advanced bug hunter and front-end web developer, with a passion for securing digital platforms and crafting seamless web interfaces.


I have successfully identified and secured vulnerabilities in prominent platforms such as Nokia, lsgkerala.gov, NIT Calicut, mahaconnect.in, gjust.ac.in, and several other government domains.

With every project, I aim to push boundaries and contribute to a safer and more efficient digital world.

Projects

DoxF

DoxF is a Python-based command-line tool designed to validate and retrieve detailed information about phone numbers. It can perform offline validation, fetch real-time data from external APIs, and display a variety of details such as the phone number's country, carrier, line type, timezones, and more. This tool is useful for verifying phone numbers and gathering related metadata for security, research, or general information purposes.
